Reception

Celebratory Entry of the Bride & Groom

You might have heard about the Bharat (groom's processional entry, typically on a horse) in North India or heard about Jaanavasam (groom's entry, typically in a car) in South India. 

We have a twist celebrating the couple entry onto the Reception stage with tons of dancing to Dhol beats. 

The celebration culminates with a garland exchange between bride and the groom.
